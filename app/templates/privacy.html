Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create a HiveFlow account, we collect:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash — we never store your actual password).
  • Profile Picture: An optional profile image you choose to upload.
  • Task Data: Tasks, projects, discussions, and comments you create within the app.
  • Google Calendar Data: If you choose to connect Google Calendar, we store OAuth tokens to sync your tasks. We only access your calendar data — nothing else in your Google account.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the HiveFlow service.
  • To send transactional emails (password resets, email verification).
  • To sync tasks with Google Calendar when you explicitly connect it.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase (AWS infrastructure).
  • All connections use TLS/SSL encryption in transit.
  • Passwords are hashed using Werkzeug's security module (PBKDF2).
  • Session cookies are secured with HttpOnly, Secure, and SameSite flags.

4. Google Calendar Integration

HiveFlow's use of Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We only request access to your Google Calendar to create, update, and delete task events. You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time from your dashboard.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data via your Profile page.
  • Update your name, email, and profile picture at any time.
  • Delete your tasks and project data.
  • Disconnect Google Calendar integration.
  • Request account deletion by contacting us at the email below.

6. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party advertising cookies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting a notice on the app.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at tamanna900620@gmail.com.
