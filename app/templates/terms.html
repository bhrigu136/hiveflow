Teams and organizations

Org admins can manage members and projects. If an admin removes you from an org, you lose access to that org's content. Your personal tasks and other orgs aren't affected.

If you connect Google Calendar, HiveFlow will create, update, and remove calendar events that correspond to your tasks. You can turn this off whenever you want. We stick to Google's guidelines on how we handle the connection.
